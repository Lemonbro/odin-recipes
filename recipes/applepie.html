Apple Pie

Description

In this recipe you will be making a delicious apple pie completely from scratch.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Green Apples
  • Nutmeg
  • Ice Water
  • Salt
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Flour work area
  2. Cut butter to cubes
  3. In a bowl add cubed butter, salt and flour
  4. Fold butter into flour while adding ice water
  5. Mix and form a ball with the dough
  6. Lay dough ball on floured work area
  7. Cut dough ball in half
  8. Flatten with rolling pin in a circle
  9. Lay flattened dough in pie pan
  10. Mix sliced apples with nutmeg, cinnamon, and sugar
  11. Add sliced apples to pan with dough layed in it
  12. Take other half of dough and flatten
  13. Lay flatten dough over top of pie
  14. Take egg whites and brush on top of pie
  15. Use fork to poke holes in the center of the dough ontop of pie
  16. Bake
  17. Let cool, and enjoy!-
