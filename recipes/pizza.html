Pizza

Description

In this recipe you will be using a premade dough to make a delicious combination pizza with your choice of toppings.

Ingredients

  • Premade Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Mushroom
  • Bell Pepper
  • Sausage
  • Red Onion
  • Cornmeal

Steps

  1. Flour work area / pizza stone generously
  2. Sprinkle cornmeal on pizza stone
  3. Preheat oven
  4. Remove dough from fridge let come to room temp or close to it
  5. Knead dough to desired pizza size
  6. Spread pizza sauce
  7. Add Cheese
  8. Add toppings generously
  9. Add more cheese
  10. Put in oven and cook
  11. Remove from oven and enjoy!
