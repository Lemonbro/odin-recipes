Burrito

Description

In this recipe you will be heating a flour tortilla and some beans and cheese to make a simple burrito. You can add pico, salsa, hot sauce. Whatever fits your tastes.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Beans
  • Cheese

Optional

  • Pico
  • Hot Sauce
  • Salsa
  • Nacho Cheese
  • Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Heat beans in sauce pan.
  2. Heat tortilla in pan.
  3. Remove tortilla from pan place on plate
  4. Spoon warm beans on warmed tortilla
  5. Add cheese / nacho cheese
  6. Add hot sauce / pico / salsa & sour cream
  7. Wrap burrito gently
  8. Place burrito in pan used to warm tortilla with the seem facing down
  9. Let burrito brown
  10. Flip and remove from pan
  11. Enjoy!
